First second: The universe expands at the spead of light, and the little dot now becomes a size of a grape fruit, and the nucular forces like electromanatic force appear.

First three minutes: Protons and neutrons atart forming and combine together to form Helium and Hydrogen nuclei. The universe is too hot to form atoms, it's a soup of nuclei, electrons, and photons.

380000 years: Electrons finally slow down enough to combine with nuclei forming atoms. Light can travel freely and the universe becomes transparent for the first time.

100 million - 500 million years: Gravity starts forming stars, which fuse hydrogen into heavier elements (carbon, oxygen, iron, etc.).

1 billion Years: A massive stars explode into supernovae, leaving behind black holes. First galaxy clusters form, shaping the large-scale structure of the universe.

9 billion years: A giant cloud of gas and dust collapses under gravity, so our Sun is born! Planets, including Earth, form from leftover debris, and our solar system form.
